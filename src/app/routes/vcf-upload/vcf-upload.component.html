VCF Information

The SMART Cancer Navigator can accept Variant Call Format (VCF) files and display them in an organized fashion, allowing patients to easily import any gene-variants that they may have into the app.


To import your VCF file, click the Upload button above. Then, click the "Try It Out!" tab to see detailed information regarding your variants.


To remove your variants from the app, simply click the Logout button.
